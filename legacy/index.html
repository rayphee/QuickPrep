Tutoring should be

Approachable

Tutoring programs nowadays are more like dreaded boot camps. Students sign up, report to class, and are forced into a mold. We're here to change that.

At QuickPrep, we see each student for who he or she is, not another machine to program. We evaluate each student to champion strengths, recognize weaknesses, and develop a path to success.

Affordable

Everyone wants his or her child to be ahead of the curve. But sometimes, being one step ahead often means being financially set back. Not with QuickPrep!

Our programs are priced reasonably so you won't have to take from your college savings just to make sure your child gets into college. We know firsthand that education is expensive as it is, so it is our mission to provide the best possible tutoring for the most sensible price.

Achievable

Many tutoring programs cram as many kids as possible into one room with a single teacher. This approach overlooks the fundamental element that every student needs to succeed— personal attention.

We make sure every student gets the attention he or she needs to achieve his or her goals. All of our individual tutoring programs have one brilliant teacher matched with your child and our group tutoring programs are capped at 15 students with 3 teachers per session. It's maintaining this personal touch that makes the difference.
