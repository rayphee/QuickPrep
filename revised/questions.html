FAQs

General

  1. How does QuickPrep differ from other tutoring centers?

    2. Unlike other tutoring services, our small number of customers allows us to offer more customized and personalized analyses of student's progress through his or her time in QuickPrep. A smaller base also means more resources available per student; each student gets direct access to a tutor who has been through the college process already. In addition, top performing students in the summer program receive complimentary access to a tutor for college admissions guidance at a reduced price.

  2. Where is QuickPrep?

    3. QuickPrep is a tutoring agency that pairs up students with tutors. Each summer, we hold a group program for the SHSAT exam at the National Opera Center's facilities, which is located at 330 7th Avenue, between 28th & 29th Streets.

  3. What sort of programs does QuickPrep offer?

    4. We offer tutoring programs for SHSAT, AP®, SAT®, SAT Subject Tests™, and ACT®. For every test, we have an individual program in which each student is paired with one of our tutors. For the SHSAT, we offer a group program each summer. For more information, please check out our programs. Note, we do not currently cater to all AP® or SAT Subject Test™ courses; for a complete list of subjects we cover on these exams, please refer to these lists: SAT Subject Tests™ AP® Exams

  4. How will QuickPrep prepare students for their exams?

    5. We initially diagnose students to see what concepts they mastered and which concepts need work. After we determine the areas students don't need help on, we shift focus to areas that students do.

    During our sessions, we first give a short lesson, followed by a practice round in which each student does practice problems with tutors. If students do not understand concepts, tutors individually assist them in the practice rounds.

    Occasionally, a session is dedicated to practice tests in which students take an exam in simulated testing conditions. This exam allows us to gauge and report student progress and further expand on what problems need to address in the next few sessions.

  5. What sort of payment plans does QuickPrep offer?

    6. We understand money does not grow on trees, so we offer several ways to pay for our programs.

    If you choose to enroll in QuickPrep's Individual Prep Programs, you may choose to pay by the session and have an invoice mailed to you or pre-pay for a certain number of sessions and receive a 10% discount if the total time exceeds 8 hours

    If you choose to enroll in QuickPrep's Group Prep Program, you may choose to pay by the session and have an invoice mailed to you, pre-pay for the entire program and receive a $160 discount, or pay half as down payment and pay the remaining balance halfway into the program.

SHSAT Summer Group Prep

  1. Do you guys have a plan/syllabus for this program?

    2. Yes, we do! You can check it out over here: SHSAT Summer Group Prep Syllabus

  2. What materials will QuickPrep use?

    3. We will be using a mix of previous year SHSAT and custom made exams for practice tests and practice questions. We will be using Barron's NYC SHSAT 3rd edition to guide our lessons and SHSAT Practice Tests: TJHSST Edition for supplementary materials and additional practice questions.

  3. How long is the summer group program?

    4. The SHSAT Summer Group Prep is a 96 Hour course that will be held from July 11 to August 18. The program will be held Monday-Thursday from 10AM to 2:30PM.

  4. How many students will be in the summer group program?

    5. Our goal at QuickPrep is to ensure a high level of personal attention for students. In order to do this, we cap our group programs at 15 students per season. We usually expect between 12-15 students per season, but in the off chance that less than 8 students register before our registration deadline, we provide the registered students with 96 hours of Individual Prep for their respective exams.

Individual Prep

  1. What sort of programs are available for Individual Prep?

    2. We offer Individual Prep for SHSAT, AP®, SAT®, SAT Subject Tests™, and ACT®. For more information, please check out our programs. Note, we do not currently cater to all AP® or SAT Subject Test™ courses; for a complete list of subjects we cover on these exams, please refer to these lists: SAT Subject Tests™ AP® Exams

  2. What subjects are covered in Individual Prep for SAT Subject Test™?

    3. We cover a broad range of subjects for SAT Subject Test™, including:

    • US History
    • World History
    • Math Level I
    • Math Level II
    • Biology E/M
    • Chemistry
    • Physics
    • Spanish

  3. What subjects are covered in Individual Prep for AP® Exams?

    4. We cover a broad range of subjects for AP® Exams, including:

    • Psychology
    • US Government & Politics
    • US History
    • World History
    • Calculus AB
    • Calculus BC
    • Biology
    • Chemistry
    • Physics C: Electricity & Magnetism
    • Physics C: Mechanics
    • Physics 1: Algebra-Based
    • Physics 2: Algebra-Based

  4. If I registered for Individual Prep, does the study session have to be at my house?

    5. No, it does not have to be at the client's house. The study session's location can be negotiated as long as your tutor agrees to the location as well. We usually try to not change the location of the study session one to two hours prior because our tutors usually leave time to commute.

  5. If I registered for Individual Prep at a certain time but I cannot make it, can I cancel/change the registration?

    6. As long as we are notified one to two hours prior, you may cancel or change the registration. You will not be billed for the cancelled registration if you were paying via invoice; however, if you were on a prepaid plan, your cancelled registration will be credited for another session.
